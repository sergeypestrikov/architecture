Courses List

In our nautical school you can get all the necessary certificates.

COASTAL SKIPPER

Advanced training course for boatmasters of non-commercial sailing and motor yachts, sufficient to manage your own or rented / charter boat anywhere in the world.

The theoretical knowledge gained, supported by subsequent practical training, will allow you to confidently walk in coastal areas of the sea with a distance of up to 50 miles

from the coast, plan long-distance sea passages, and also operate ship systems to the level of medium repair.

BAREBOAT SKIPPER

A comprehensive training course for captains of sailing and motor yachts is sufficient to manage a cruise yacht, rented / charter anywhere in the world.

The program of the course is phased, consists of theoretical lessons, including navigation laying on maps, a practical training course on the school's cruising yachts

and qualifying sailing to obtain a sailing qualification of 200 miles.

YACHTMASTER OFFSHORE

Theoretical training course for captains of cruising yachts.

After training, you will be able to command a sailing and motor non-commercial vessel without sailing restrictions in the navigation area up to 200 miles from the place of refuge,

having legal Russian state documents all over the world.

COMPETENT CREW

The purpose of the course is to gain theoretical knowledge and practical skills that allow a novice yachtsman to join any crew of a sports sailing vessel.

NAUTICAL ASTRONOMY

In the process of studying on the course "Nautical Astronomy" you will gain practical skills in determining the geographical coordinates of a yacht's position on the high seas using celestial bodies,

using astronavigational instruments and ephemeris tables with an accuracy of 1-2 miles.

Learn to calculate the time of sunrise and sunset, the time of the beginning of civil and nautical twilight, determine the compass correction at sunrise or the North Star, determine the phases and age of the moon,

as a factor in the illumination of the navigation area.

Get an idea about the celestial sphere, the main points and planes and the principles of the location and movement of celestial bodies on the sphere,

understand the connection between celestial and geographical coordinates, as well as understand the differences between ship, local, standard, Greenwich and sidereal time.
